## 1a 概念态(narrative-review,带三层问题读)

- **理论层 Q1(φ 统一形态)**:无公认闭式。最接近第一性原理的只到 occupancy 层(fitness ∝ 抗原捕获概率 = Langmuir/Michaelis 占据 p=[Ag]/([Ag]+K_D),Hill n=1);occupancy 之上(capture→proliferation→reproductive fitness)全是 ad hoc(logistic/soft-threshold/exp/Hill 自由 n)。即 φ「occupancy 以下机制性,以上唯象」,接缝处正是让步聚集处。主流脚手架=G→P→fitness,P→fitness 用非参单调非线性拟合(global epistasis),常 sigmoid 但少承诺闭式。
- **理论层 Q2(单序列 f 能否载群体相对性)**:能,且是已解建模问题,非矛盾。f(s) 是 f(s;**x**) 的简写,**x**=群体态。框架:replicator(增长=绝对 fitness 减群体均值)、soft/hard selection(Wallace/Levene;soft=deme 内 rank/归一化,正是「competitive-relative」形式家;hard=绝对,才是单序列 f 字面成立处)、frequency-dependent、GC 竞争(B 细胞争有限抗原/T 帮助,p_i=a_i/Σa_j 或 softmax)。**干净解**:φ 在表型层定义为「给定竞争者的期望捕获」,把群体依赖收进 mean-field「场」变量 θ(**x**)(总结合容量/竞争者均亲和),f(s;θ(**x**))低维,既保单序列形又诚实编码群体依赖=带 mean-field 竞争项的 soft selection。
- **理论层 Q3(∘ 单通道在多维表型下)**:仅相对于**完整**表型向量成立;对 affinity-alone 失效。是充分统计假设:phenotype 是 fitness 的充分统计量。完整(affinity/stability/expression/polyreactivity 全收)则 fitness⊥sequence|完整 p;漏一个 fitness-relevant 轴、或序列绕过表型直达 fitness(免疫原 motif/体内清除)即破。多维特别威胁单通道因选择作用于**合取**(developability=stability AND 低 polyreactivity AND expression)。polyreactivity 是典范隐藏通道。

## 1a 验证证据:辨识层「真上限 vs 方法天花板」裁决(决定性分叉)

- **裁决倾向**:monotone+scale 在「origin-fixation / mutation-selection 观测模型」下是**真结构上限**(从经典群体遗传学 Kimura fixation prob / Halpern-Bruno scaled fitness 演绎可证),非 CoSiNE/DASM 经验天花板。但关键 refinement:**该上限条件于观测的是 fixation/substitution 结局**;若改观测带绝对 φ 信息的量(clone-size/abundance 动力学、birth-death 速率、moderate-2Nes 强 drift 区的时间分辨频率幅值),additive constant 与 scale 可变得可辨识。**突破杠杆=换观测对象,不是同数据上换更好估计器。**
- 证据偏向真上限(强):① 不确定性在 likelihood 里非估计器——CoSiNE Eq.(4) 把可观测转移率写成 k·µ·Pfix,µ 已知,数据只约束 k·Pfix,Pfix 只依赖 sxy=Fy−Fx(只见差不见绝对、且总与 Ne 共乘)。任何估计器同模型同墙。② Halpern-Bruno scaled fitness 2Ne(Fj−Fi)=典范证明,fitness 只作为 Ne·f 乘积与位点内 aa 差可辨识。③ Bollback 2008:时序频率只能估 2Nes 乘积,Ne→∞ 无力分离。④ Pfix 严格单调于 s(Kimura 1962),任何保序单调重参 likelihood 不变 ⇒ φ 仅可辨识到单调变换(演绎非经验)。⑤ 两 SOTA 独立收敛同 workaround(DASM gauge-fix;CoSiNE 任意 k+加常 C+rank-only eval)。
- 证据偏向方法天花板(弱但真,=突破钩):① 上限条件于 origin-fixation 观测模型。② Ne-s 在 moderate-2Nes/强 drift 区可分(Bollback/Jewett-Steinrücken-Song);GC 是小群体强 drift 区,潜在利于破 scale 简并。③ 回归地形推断有偏但可表征/可校(Otwinowski-Plotkin 2014),非不可能。

## E-理论层(φ 形态 / 群体相对 / ∘ 单通道)

**E-T1 [SBI/Ralph 2025 arXiv:2508.09871,作者原文]** φ 未知:
> "the exact form of this relationship between affinity and fitness, which we call the 'affinity-fitness response function', is not known"
> "However, the details of the relationship between affinity and fitness are unknown."

**E-T2 [SBI/Ralph 2025,作者原文]** φ 体内不可测:
> "the fitness of a B cell is an emergent property of an intact, functioning germinal center and thus cannot be measured using an in vitro assay"

**E-T3 [SBI/Ralph 2025,作者原文]** 单调性是强加非导出:
> "In order for the GC reaction to improve affinity, this relationship must be increasing."

**E-T4 [SBI/Ralph 2025,作者原文]** 形态假设(非 shape-free):
> "all simulations in this paper use a sigmoid on affinity x";该 sigmoid "cannot ... accommodate either a decrease in fitness with affinity" 且 "requires symmetry around its midpoint"

**E-T5 [SBI/Ralph 2025,作者原文]** ∘ 单通道 / 无 epistasis 假设:
> "we simply add the effect of each individual mutation (i.e. we assume no epistasis)"

**E-T6 [SBI/Ralph 2025,作者原文]** 群体均一假设:
> "we assume that all GCs have the same response function"

**E-T7 [Otwinowski 2018 PNAS,作者原文]** φ 候选形态=承重假设:
> "The key assumption is that mutations contribute in a simple way to some unobserved trait, which is related to the observed trait by a nonlinear mapping."
> "The assumption underlying this model is that mutations affect the measured phenotype only via a single unobserved additive trait."

**E-T8 [Otwinowski 2018 PNAS,作者原文]** 单调 φ 的代价(禁 sign epistasis),且形态是 parsimony 选择:
> "Because we assume that the nonlinear relationship is monotonically increasing with increasing values of the additive underlying trait, the resulting model is single peaked, and it shows no sign epistasis."
> "we choose a flexible parametric family of monotonic functions, I-splines, to avoid fitting an arbitrarily complex model"
> "our strategy is based on the gambit that ... a simple-to-understand model of global epistasis can account for most phenotypic variation."

**E-T9 [Otwinowski 2018 MBE 35:2345,作者原文]** 单 trait 形态机制性失效边界:
> "these approaches assume a single additive intermediate trait and a monotonic nonlinear relation ... The only thermodynamic model that fits into this framework is a two state model with an additive energy, whereas a three state model is necessary to predict fold stability and explain the observed epistasis of GB1."

**E-T10 [Phillips 2021 CR9114,作者原文]** 统一 φ 试过即弃,退 per-antigen ad-hoc 高阶:
> "We explored the possibility of 'global' epistasis by inferring a nonlinear transformation of the -logK D values ... but found that this approach did not significantly reduce the order or number of specific interaction coefficients needed to explain the data."

**E-T11 [Phillips 2021,作者原文]** 亲和力当时间 proxy + 选择只建模为 −logK_D 单调函数:
> "Considering the maximum –logK D achieved by sequences with a given number of mutations (a proxy for time)..."
> (CH65 同框架原话)"we implement a framework in which the probability of any mutational step is higher if -log K D increases and lower if -log K D decreases"

**E-T12 [Phillips 2021,作者原文]** 体内演化无法确定 + 物理相关窗外推断 pin 边界:
> "We note that we cannot conclusively determine how CR9114 and CR6261 evolved in vivo."

**E-T13 [Phillips 2021,作者原文]** 第二表型通道(表达)承认后搁置:
> "Antibody expression is not strongly impacted by sequence identity, although some mutations have modest effects that may be inversely correlated with their effect on affinity"

**E-T14 [Phillips/CH65 eLife 83628 同 lab 作者回复]** 单通道局限自承:
> "our study is limited to examining antigen affinity and neglects other properties that are known to impact antibody affinity maturation (e.g., autoreactivity)"

**E-T15 [CR9114 eLife 71393 Reviewer #2 公开评审]** 可溶 HA 亲和 ≠ fitness,polyreactivity 正交通道:
> "it is unclear what the natural selection pressures are ... not straightforwardly related to improved breadth of binding to soluble HA ... The known polyreactivity of many bNAbs ... is another confounding factor."

**E-T16 [Adams/Tite-Seq 2016 eLife,作者原文]** 方法靠剔除其他通道隔离亲和(=普通选择/DMS 见的不是纯亲和):
> "The measurement of titration curves eliminates the confounding effects of antibody expression and stability that arise in standard deep mutational scanning assays."

**E-T17 [Adams 2019 Cell Systems,作者原文]** 测量量↔自由能等价是「相信」非证明;φ 标度选来最小化 epistasis:
> "While our analysis of epistasis strictly applies to that measured phenotype and not directly to the free energy, these two quantities are believed to be equivalent (VanAntwerp and Wittrup, 2000)."
> "We show that representing affinity as the binding free energy minimizes epistasis."

**E-T18 [Jain 2017 PNAS,作者原文]** fitness 多轴合取,亲和不充分:
> "achieving drug-like properties at the same time is an additional requirement"
> "no single unfavorable assay we examined definitively predicted failure to advance to at least phase-2 clinical trials"
> (Arsiwala 2025 后继)"a dozen interacting but distinct attributes—are inherent to an antibody amino acid sequence."

## E-辨识层(f 仅到 monotone+scale:真上限 vs 方法天花板)

**E-I1 [CoSiNE arXiv:2602.18982 Eq.(4),作者原文]** 任意标量 k:
> "we decompose Qxy ... as: Qxy = k µxy Pfix(x→y) ... where ... k is an arbitrary scalar."

**E-I2 [CoSiNE Eq.(5),作者原文]** 恢复分数=log Pfix + 未定加性常数 C:
> "Score(x→y) = log pθ(y|x, t) − log q(y|x, t) ≈ log(Pfix(x→y)) + C"

**E-I3 [CoSiNE §D.3 / §4.1,作者原文]** monotone-only,只用 Spearman 验证:
> "Crucially, the fixation probability expression is monotonic with respect to sxy, which explains why the selection score ... shows strong Spearman correlation with empirical relative fitness measurements."
> "we rely on standard population genetics theory (Kimura, 1962) to show that Pfix(x→y) is monotonic with respect to sxy"

**E-I4 [CoSiNE Eq.(15),作者原文]** fitness 只作差、且与 Ne 共乘:
> "Pfix(x→y) = (1−e^−2sxy)/(1−e^−2Nesxy), where ... sxy = Fy − Fx is the selective advantage ... and Ne is the effective population size."

**E-I5 [CoSiNE,作者原文]** 只证近似误差界,不证辨识:
> CoSiNE 仅给 "an error bound that is quadratic in branch length"(Prop 4.1 / D.1),对绝对 fitness 辨识零证明。

**E-I6 [DASM bioRxiv 2025.10.21.683652,作者原文]** 显式 unidentifiability + gauge:
> "it gives us a 'gauge' that eliminates a potential unidentifiability in the model."
> "We cannot use perplexity because selection factors alone do not give likelihoods."
> "this approach effectively fixes the selection factors for neutral substitutions to be 1"

**E-I7 [DASM,作者原文]** 信号被突变过程 confound;分数本身只到线性变换:
> "However, this desired signal is confounded by the preceding factors."
> masked-marginals 变体分数等于 log-prob 差 "up to a linear transformation" / "up to an additive constant"。

**E-I8 [Halpern-Bruno 1998 MBE 15:910,典范结论(综述转述)]** 速率只经 scaled coef 2Ne(Fj−Fi),per-site aa fitness 仅相对可辨识(需 gauge)。

**E-I9 [Bollback-York-Nielsen 2008 Genetics 179:497,作者原文]** 只 2Nes 乘积可估,Ne→∞ 无力:
> "as Ne becomes large, the trajectory of the allele frequency through time becomes approximately deterministic and there should be little or no power to estimate Ne."

**E-I10 [Morlon 2022 / gcdyn Ralph 2026,作者原文]** birth-death 速率从系统树本难辨识(=可换观测的暗示):
> "identifiability of birth and death rates from phylogenies is inherently difficult"

**E-I11 [Otwinowski-Plotkin 2014 PNAS 111:E2301]** 回归地形推断有偏(=方法天花板证据,可校正)。

## E-容器层(离散 CTMC 是否正确数学对象 / 扩散极限边界)

**E-C1 [Patwa-Wahl 2008 J R Soc Interface,作者原文]** 三容器分立、扩散非严格极限只是弱选择近似:
> "there are three approaches ... a Markov chain approach can determine the fixation probability exactly ... typically feasible only when the population size is quite small ... methods based on discrete branching processes ... any branching process approach provides an approximation ... assumes that the wild-type population is sufficiently large that the fate of each mutant allele is independent ... when the population is large and the change in gene frequency is small in each generation (i.e. selection is weak), methods that incorporate a diffusion approximation may be used."

**E-C2 [Patwa-Wahl 2008,作者原文]** Haldane 2s 靠隐藏后代分布假设;P_fix 对生活史极端敏感:
> "Haldane's elegant result necessarily relies on a number of simplifying assumptions."
> "individuals in such a branching process cannot die before having offspring ... there is no room in Haldane's approach to independently specify a survival probability."
> "highlighted the extreme sensitivity of fixation probabilities to such assumptions."
> "assuming that all mutations confer a fecundity advantage leads to an overestimate of the order 2 ln(2)∼1.4 for generation time mutations."

**E-C3 [Park-Krug 2007 PNAS,作者原文]** 单谱系/SSWM 在 Nμ≫1 必破:
> "First, it neglects the occurrence of multiple mutations ... plausible only when Nμ is small ... but it must surely break down in the CI regime where Nμ ≫ 1. Second, the survival of a mutation against genetic drift is assumed to be independent of its success in the clonal competition process."
> "the standard theory fails in this regime."

**E-C4 [Desai-Fisher 2007 Genetics,作者原文]** SSWM vs interference 显式边界 + 随机/确定性分离假设:
> "When beneficial mutations are rare, they accumulate by a series of selective sweeps. But when they are common ... these different mutant lineages interfere and not all can fix simultaneously."
> "We assume the double mutants never arise before the single-mutant subpopulation has established ... this will be true unless mutation rates are extremely high or selection is very weak."
> "a subpopulation must become large enough to neglect random drift before it is too large to ignore saturation ... we assume this condition holds."

**E-C5 [Wilke 2005 综述,作者原文]** quasispecies 误差阈值是确定性/无限群体效应:
> "The first important point ... is that it is a deterministic effect ... even in an infinite population the error threshold occurs at a finite mutation rate."
> "this conclusion holds only when two assumptions are met: (i) The population is infinite ... (ii) Selection is soft ..."

**E-C6 [Bull-Meyers-Lachmann PMC1317512,作者原文]** 误差灾难需无限时间 + 全突变体续复制:
> "competition between the wild type and mutants ... continues to steady state conditions, a requirement that is truly satisfied only at infinite times."

**E-C7 [Dalmau 2014 arXiv:1404.2133,作者原文]** ★离散 Moran→Eigen 的严格极限是**确定性 ODE**(经 Kurtz 定理),非 diffusion SDE:
> "We prove that a Moran model converges in probability to Eigen's quasispecies model in the infinite population limit ... by means of a theorem due to Kurtz, which gives sufficient conditions for the convergence ... to a deterministic trajectory, characterised by a system of differential equations."

**E-C8 [Chalub-Souza 2011 arXiv:1107.1549,作者原文]** 离散 WF 的连续极限非唯一 diffusion(可双曲/对流-扩散,取决标度):
> "an approximation of the discrete process in the joint large population, small time-steps and weak selection limit ... The equations ... can be purely diffusive, purely hyperbolic or of convection–diffusion type ... The particular outcome will depend on the assumed scalings."

**E-C9 [Tataru 2017 Syst Biol,作者原文]** ★扩散极限硬性要求 s=O(1/N):
> "the diffusion limit to the Wright–Fisher model requires that the parameters ... u, v, m, s, and sh, are all in the order of 1/(2N) ... These might be expected to break down in cases in which the diffusion is not appropriate ... moment-based approximations might be more appropriate when the evolutionary pressure is strong."

**E-C10 [Der-Epstein-Plotkin 2011 TPB,作者原文]** WF/扩散非普适,非扩散极限定性不同:
> "the Wright–Fisher model (or its diffusion limit) is not appropriate in all circumstances."
> "many GWF processes do not converge to Kimura's diffusion ... we can classify GWF processes by the distinct continuum limits they attain."
> "fixation is virtually assured in large populations, even for only moderately advantageous alleles."

**E-C11 [Chalub-Souza 2016 J Math Biol 72:283,作者原文]** 强选择下大群体长期动力学与无限群体差异大:
> "outside of the weak-selection regime, the long-term dynamics of large populations can have very little resemblance to the infinite population case."

## 卡点全集(locked format,round-1)

### 理论层

**[理论] B-T1 φ 无机制导出的统一形态(occupancy 以下机制性,以上唯象)**
- E: E-T1(form not known)、E-T4(sigmoid 假设)、E-T7/T8(monotone single-trait 是「gambit」、I-spline 为避免拟合任意复杂模型)、E-T10(Phillips 试 global epistasis 失败,退 per-antigen 五阶)。
- S: 每个方法靠便利/parsimony 选 φ 形态,无一从机制导出;唯一尝试统一形态(global epistasis)在 CR9114 上不足以降阶。
- W(根因): 缺一套把 φ 四特征(地板/天花板/竞争/随机)连到单一机制 generator 的理论。Langmuir occupancy 层有第一性原理,但 capture→reproductive fitness 段无约束 ⇒ φ 形态被第一性原理欠定。
- 验真(挡路性): 没有从 GC 机制导出的 φ 形态类,任何恢复都把「模型误设」与「真不可恢复」混为一谈——removing it(导出 φ 形态类)才能说清在恢复什么。**满足挡路性**。非伪卡点(其可发表伪装=「提出新 φ 参数化」,但挡路版本是「无法区分误设与不可恢复」,是真卡点)。

**[理论] B-T2 群体相对性无法写进单序列 f(现有方法靠假设群体均一回避)**
- E: E-T6(assume all GCs same response function)、E-T5(assume no epistasis)、Harel 2026(选择 context/population-dependent)、1a-Q2(soft selection / mean-field 场变量可解)。
- S: 群体依赖要么被忽略(单一 response function),要么被假设掉;无算子形式显式携带群体态 θ(x)。
- W(根因): f=φ∘a 把 fitness 写成 per-sequence 函数,结构上无法在不引入显式群体态参数下容纳 frequency-dependent 项;replicator/soft-selection 在群遗有解,但抗体选择算子领域无一实例化,全靠均一假设塌缩。
- 验真: GC B 细胞争有限抗原/T 帮助(竞争为真),单序列 f 系统性把竞争效应误归为内禀 φ。removing it(带显式 mean-field 竞争场的算子)改变 φ 的定义本身。**满足挡路性**。

**[理论] B-T3 ∘ 单通道(fitness⊥sequence|phenotype)在 affinity-only 下失效(隐藏通道:polyreactivity/stability/expression)**
- E: E-T13(表达反相关)、E-T14(neglects autoreactivity)、E-T15(reviewer:polyreactivity confound)、E-T16(Tite-seq 须剔除 expression/stability ⇒ 普通选择见得到它们)、E-T18(Jain:多轴,无单一 assay 预测)、E-T9(GB1 需 3-state,单 trait 可证破 rank-preservation)。
- S: affinity-only φ 漏掉 fitness-relevant 通道;现实多表型下条件独立性经验为假。
- W(根因): ∘ 编码充分性主张(phenotype 是 fitness 充分统计量)。affinity-only 表型下主张为假——真表型向量测不全,序列保留直达 fitness 信息 ⇒ 通道泄漏。
- 验真: 单通道假设下恢复 φ 但真相是多通道,会把 polyreactivity 驱动的选择记到 affinity 头上 ⇒ 系统性错 φ。removing it(多通道算子或显式充分性检验)是诚实恢复的前提。**满足挡路性**。

### 辨识层

**[辨识] B-I1 monotone+scale 上限是「观测模型」的而非「信息」的(observable-conditional limit)**
- E: E-I1(任意 k)、E-I2(score=logPfix+C)、E-I3(monotone,Spearman-only)、E-I4(只见差 Fy−Fx,与 Ne 共乘)、E-I9(只 2Nes 可估)、E-I10(birth-death 从树本难辨识)。
- S: 两 SOTA 都把可观测建模为 fixation/substitution 结局,结局只携 Pfix 的序信息 ⇒ φ 仅到 monotone+scale;但这是观测模型属性,非数据信息上限。
- W(根因): origin-fixation likelihood 让 fitness 只经 Pfix(Fy−Fx) 进入,Pfix 严格单调 ⇒ 任何保序单调重参 likelihood 不变(演绎)。加性常数与全局尺度 Ne 在确定性/大 Ne 极限不可分。**但**:换观测对象(abundance/timing/clone-size,moderate-2Nes 强 drift 区)可让绝对 φ 进入 likelihood。
- 验真: 这是决定突破有无空间的分叉本身。若误判为「信息上限」则放弃换观测对象的整条突破路;若识别为「观测模型上限」则开辟「换 likelihood 而非换估计器」的突破空间。removing it(厘清是哪种上限并据此设计携绝对-φ 信息的观测)直接决定能否超越 CoSiNE/DASM。**满足挡路性,且可能是最深卡点**。

**[辨识] B-I2 现有 SOTA 把结构上限当方法天花板默认接受(rank-only eval + gauge-fix 把不可辨识藏起来)**
- E: E-I3(Spearman-only validation)、E-I5(只证近似误差界不证辨识)、E-I6(gauge 消 unidentifiability)、E-I7(分数只到线性变换)。
- S: CoSiNE 用 rank 相关验证、DASM 用 gauge-fix,都在「不挑战上限」前提下操作,把 monotone+scale 当既定。
- W(根因): 领域无人区分「这上限是我方法选的 likelihood 造成的」vs「这是 affinity-maturation 数据的信息极限」;两 SOTA 独立收敛同 workaround 强化了「上限是真的」的默认。
- 验真: 此默认若不被打破,所有后续方法都在 monotone 等价类内打转,绝对刻度恢复被先验排除。removing it(显式证明上限的条件性 + 给出突破观测设计)解锁绝对-φ 恢复。**满足挡路性**。注:B-I1 与 B-I2 是同一根因的「理论侧/方法社会学侧」两面,相2 发散时可合可分,此处穷举保留两条。

### 容器层

**[容器] B-C1 离散 CTMC 的严格连续极限是确定性 ODE 不是 diffusion SDE(embedding-SDE 无严格地基)**
- E: E-C7(Dalmau:Moran→Eigen 经 Kurtz 收敛到确定性 ODE)、E-C8(Chalub-Souza:连续极限可双曲/对流-扩散,取决标度)、E-C9(扩散极限硬要求 s=O(1/N))、E-C10(GWF 多数不收敛到 Kimura diffusion)。
- S: 把 φ 恢复架在连续 embedding-SDE 上(若有人这么做)缺严格极限支撑;严格极限要么是确定性 ODE(无 drift),要么需 s=O(1/N) 弱选择。
- W(根因): CTMC→SDE 不是该过程的严格极限;锚定对象已承认这点(故采离散 CTMC)。但这同时意味着:能严格导出的连续对象(确定性 ODE)丢掉了有限群体 drift——而 GC 是小群体强 drift,两个严格极限(确定性 ODE / 弱选择 diffusion)都不覆盖 GC 实际 regime。
- 验真: 若在错误容器上恢复 φ,恢复出的 φ 携带容器误设的伪影,无法归因到真 φ。removing it(确认 GC regime 下正确的数学对象)是恢复有意义的前提。**满足挡路性**。

**[容器] B-C2 μ·P_fix 标量化丢生活史信息(P_fix 非 fitness 的函数,对后代分布极端敏感)**
- E: E-C2(Haldane 2s 靠隐藏后代分布假设;P_fix 对生活史极端敏感;等适合度突变 π 差 2ln2≈1.4 倍)、E-I4(fitness 只作差进 Pfix)。
- S: Q(s→s')=μ·P_fix(Δf) 把容器的选择部分塌缩成 Δf 的标量函数,但 P_fix 真实依赖生活史(fecundity vs generation-time)非仅 Δf。
- W(根因): P_fix(Δf) 假设 fitness 经单一标量 Δf 完全决定固定概率;真实固定概率依赖后代分布/生活史维度 ⇒ 同 Δf 不同生活史给不同 P_fix。容器把这压成一维,把生活史差异误吸收进 Δf 的估计。
- 验真: 恢复 φ 时,生活史变异被错记为 φ 的形状 ⇒ 污染。removing it(P_fix 显式含生活史维度,或验证 GC 下标量化可接受)是诚实恢复前提。**满足挡路性**。

**[容器] B-C3 单谱系 CTMC 在 Nμ≫1(克隆干涉)下破,GC 正处该 regime**
- E: E-C3(Park-Krug:Nμ≫1 必破,drift-竞争独立性假设错)、E-C4(Desai-Fisher:beneficial 常见时谱系干涉不能同时固定)、Harel 2026(GC 选择群体依赖)。
- S: Q(s→s') 单谱系尺度假设突变逐个固定、谱系独立;GC 中多谱系并发竞争抗原。
- W(根因): 单谱系/SSWM 要求 Nμ≪1;GC 是突变充分+竞争密集(Nμ 不小)⇒ 多谱系并发,P_fix 的边际单谱系值高估、且 s_eff(t)=s−s̄(t) 非平稳可变负。容器在错误 regime ⇒ 选择信号被克隆干涉扭曲。
- 验真: 单谱系容器恢复的 φ 把克隆干涉效应误归 φ ⇒ 系统性偏。removing it(多谱系/measure-valued 或 interacting branching 容器)改变恢复的数学对象。**满足挡路性**。与 B-T2(群体相对性)同源于「群体/竞争」,但 B-T2 在理论层(算子形式)、B-C3 在容器层(随机过程对象),分立保留。

## 卡点全集 round-2 新增(saturation 第 1 轮 → 产新卡点,未饱和)

**[辨识+理论] B-X1 确定性-φ 恢复是误设;φ 的噪声律本身不可恢复(noise-law-non-identifiability)**
- E: DeWitt/Victora 2025 bioRxiv 10.1101/2025.06.02.656870「GCs achieve predictable outcomes by means of **noisy but persistent selection**」;SBI 108880「on average, tend to have more offspring ... exact form ... is not known」;drift barrier eLife 93629「if the selection coefficient is sufficiently weak relative to drift ... alleles behave as if they are effectively neutral ... drift barrier」。
- S: 所有 SOTA 恢复目标是条件均值 E[fitness|affinity],把其余塞进「噪声」;但 φ 被描述为内禀随机(noisy)。φ 的方差/高阶矩律从不建模,却支配动力学(决定哪些 affinity 差对选择可见)。
- W(根因): φ 是条件分布 P(fitness|affinity) 非函数;SOTA 静默塌缩到一阶矩。两失败:(i) 误设——「恢复 φ」未声明目标是均值响应还是全律时即 ill-posed,均值可辨、噪声律不可辨;(ii) 噪声地板——dφ/da 给的 per-cell 选择差低于 drift barrier(≈1/N_eff)处,信号**可证**不可恢复(随机项压倒,选择不可辨于 drift)。区别于 B-I1(monotone+scale 是干净确定性数据上的全局 gauge):这是方差驱动的**局部信息地板**,rank 数据再多也去不掉。
- 验真: 形式化 φ 为分布并显式建模噪声项,可(a)给良定恢复目标(b)算出 drift-barrier 蕴含的「盲带」affinity 区间(c)不再把确定性曲线当全对象上报。把隐藏误设变成量化辨识边界=直接进展。**满足挡路性**。

**[辨识/容器] B-X2 ★存活/采样算子未建模且不可逆;观测 f 是 φ 的扭曲投影(survivorship-projection)——触及锚定对象**
- E: DeWitt/Victora 2025「apparent features ... **such as permissiveness to low-affinity lineages and rapid plateauing of affinity are likely artifacts of survivorship biases that distort our view**」;SBI 108880「**'push of the past' and the 'pull of the present' ... can mislead interpretation** of germinal center sequence data ... an approach is needed that can **model extinct lineages that do not appear in the reconstructed tree**」;Matsen blog 2025-11-28「only includes lineages that survived ... **a highly biased subpopulation** ... permissive ... **due to those low-affinity cells not yet being purged**」。
- S: 两个最被引的「φ 性质」(permissive to low-affinity、early plateau)根本不是 φ 的性质,是只观测采样时存活者的伪影。
- W(根因): 模型写成 f=φ∘a,但数据由 σ(f) 生成,σ=存活-采样算子(灭绝谱系缺席、当下细胞过度代表)。σ 是多对一投影、**不可逆**——不同 φ 给相同存活快照。真观测模型是 **f=σ∘φ∘a**;直接把 φ∘a 拟合存活数据,无论容器多好都有偏。8 条 round-1 卡点无一含 σ。
- 验真: removing it=把 σ 加进模型(likelihood 条件于谱系存活到采样时;用时序/go-back-in-time 采样破投影)。replay 程序证明这能恢复一致 φ 而存活-only 不能 ⇒ 卡点承重且可移除。**满足挡路性,且修改锚定对象 f=φ∘a → f=σ∘φ∘a:触发 Global Backtrack 审视(见下)**。

**[辨识] B-X3 φ 的地板/饱和创造平坦区=局部信息全失(structural-flat-region-info-hole)**
- E: Erdoğan 2024 Nat Commun s41467-024-55012-4「a **selectively neutral zone (i.e., a fitness plateau) exists above the selection threshold**」「**Threshold-like selection ... promotes survival of variants that exhibit equal fitness**」「extent of phenotypic variation will depend on the width of the fitness plateau ... can be very large (>100-fold ...)」。
- S: φ 有≥2 个零梯度区:affinity 地板下全清除(无存活=无数据)、阈值上中性平台(>100 倍表型差等 fitness)。选择信息只存于窄过渡带。
- W(根因): dφ/da=0 处数据对 φ 形状零约束——非 gauge 模糊(B-I1)而是信息局部不存在。任何「平台够平、地板够致死」的曲线等价拟合。区别于 B-X1 随机地板(斜率非零但被噪声淹)。地板与 B-X2 复合:地板下变体死 ⇒ σ 抹掉约束 φ 下臂所需的区域。
- 验真: removing it=(a)显式把 φ 可辨支集限到过渡带、把地板/平台声明为「形状无约束」而非在那拟合伪结构;(b)设计把变体置于过渡带的 assay(replay 作者:用 lower starting affinity 抗原可 map 早期动力学)。命名这洞=精确告知新数据该投哪=进展。**满足挡路性**。

**[容器] B-X4 已知-M 是构造上 selection-free 的;活体编码底物上 μ 与 φ 不可分(mutation-selection-non-separability)**
- E: Yaari 2013 S5F Front Immunol「SHM displays intrinsic biases that can confound statistical analysis」「these intrinsic biases can **give the appearance of selection**」「**To avoid the confounding influences of selection**, most existing models are built using mutation data from **intronic regions ... and non-productively rearranged Ig genes** ... **Focusing on positions where only synonymous mutations were possible removes the confounding influence of selection**」。
